# About

Tech Event Tracker is a personal index of **on-site technology events** &mdash; conferences, summits, expos, and developer gatherings &mdash; organised into two tabs:

- **Netherlands** &mdash; events held in NL.
- **Europe** &mdash; events held elsewhere in Europe.

Each tab lists upcoming events with the **soonest at the top**. Past events drop off automatically.

## How it's made

Entries are curated with AI assistance from publicly listed event pages and conference aggregators. Each entry shows a short description plus a link to the official site and, where available, a direct ticket link. Always confirm dates and ticketing on the official site before booking travel.

## Caveats

- Dates can shift; venues can change. The official site is always the source of truth.
- &ldquo;On-site&rdquo; here means physical, in-person attendance. Hybrid events are included only if the on-site track is meaningful.
- The list is not exhaustive &mdash; it focuses on widely-recognised, mid-to-large events plus a curated handful of niche ones.
